Description

Greek lentil soup, also called Fakes soupa, has simple ingredients and is full of protein and iron. It’s super healthy, easy to make and tastes incredible! This fakes recipe is so comforting, it reminds me of my childhood. It was a staple growing up and still remains one today.

It is traditionally served with a drizzle of olive oil and lots of vinegar. Though the vinegar is of course optional, try it! It lifts the lentils and adds another dimension of flavor!

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Veggie broth
  • Seasonings
    • Bay Leaf
    • Oregano
    • Red Vinegar

Steps

  1. Rinse and partially cook the lentils. Strain and set aside.
  2. Sauté the veggies and garlic.
  3. Add the lentils, tomato sauce, and spices.
  4. Mix in the broth and bay leaves, then simmer and enjoy!
